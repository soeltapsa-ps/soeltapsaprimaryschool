Admissions

We welcome applications from students who are eager to learn and grow. Our admissions process ensures that we provide the best possible environment for all learners.

How to Apply

  1. Download the admission form.
  2. Fill out the form and prepare required documents.
  3. Submit the form in person or via email.

Download Admission Forms

  • Download SPS Admission Form (PDF)
  • Download ECCD Admission Form (PDF)

Required Documents

  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Recent passport-size photographs (2)
  • Previous academic records
  • Medical certificate (if available)

Note: Please make sure all required documents are ready before filling the online application form. After submitting online, kindly visit the school office with the original documents for verification.
